Accessibility statement

Cambridge Stand Up To Racism is committed to making our website accessible to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or technology.

Our approach

  • The site uses clear text, simple layouts, and high-contrast colours to improve readability.
  • All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text.
  • The website can be navigated using a keyboard and works with common screen readers.
  • Links are underlined and clearly identifiable by colour and style.

Reporting accessibility issues

If you experience any problems accessing information on this site or need it in a different format, please email us at standuptoracism.cambridge@gmail.com.

Compatibility

The site is designed to work with recent versions of all major web browsers on both desktop and mobile devices. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standard.
