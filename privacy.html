Privacy Policy

Cambridge Stand Up To Racism (“we”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy.

What data we collect

  • Mailing list sign-ups: When you subscribe, we collect your name, email address, and any optional details you provide (e.g. phone-number, regional area, volunteering interests).
  • Donations: If you use the "donate" buttons, your donation is handled securely through Stripe and your payment information is handled directly by Stripe; we do not see or store your card details.
  • Cookies: This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools.

How we use your data

We use your data only to send campaign updates and information about events and actions organised by Stand Up To Racism Cambridge.

Who has access

Your data is stored and processed through Mailchimp and Stripe, which comply with UK and EU data protection laws. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe or ask for your data to be deleted at any time by emailing standuptoracism.cambridge@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this notice, please contact us at the email address above.
