Other ways to help

  • Based in Peterborough, Ely or Cambridge? Join us at one of our upcoming events, there are lots of ways to offer valuable support.
  • Fill in our volunteer form below, and let us know how you could help - just a couple of hours a month makes a real difference.
  • Activities include: delivering leaflets in your local area, phoning new members, admin (printing leaflets, organizing leafletting rounds, events), organising people and projects, graphic design, social media, writing, video recording and editing, and more...
  • Or maybe you have other skills or ways you'd like to help that aren't listed here? We'd love to hear from you!
  • If you do have some time but aren't quite sure what you could do, just put "not sure" and fill in your phone-number and we can have a chat.
