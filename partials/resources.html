Online Resources & Training

  • "Hope Not Hate" have an excellent set of community resources for local councillors and grass-roots organisations who are organising against the far-right.
  • "Organizing for Power" run highly-impactful free online training for groups of 10 or more. Their "Power-Up" course consists of four online sessions (2-hours each) starting on 18 & 20 November and concluding on 2 & 4 December.
  • "Social Movement Technologies" are a nonprofit/NGO providing organising strategy, training and campaign support to build people power and win in the digital age. They run online training, and the quality of their free training is outstanding.
